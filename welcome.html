Thank you for installing BetterRead!

We hope by using BetterRead, we can make your internet browsing experience easier. The following is short quick-start guide to using BetterRead.

How to use:

  • On any webpage that you want read to you press Ctrl+Shift+S (Command on Mac)
  • You can also click on the BetterRead logo next to the address bar and click the "Speak Page" button
  • While the page is being read, you can press Ctrl+Shift+S or the "Speak Page" button again to stop the narration
  • You can change how the page is read to you and the keybind to activate the narration in the settings page
  • You can access the options page again by clicking the BetterRead logo and click the "Options" button

Note: Pages that start with chrome://, edge://, or about: cannot be read by BetterRead, sorry!
